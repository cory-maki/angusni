About

A Practice Built Between Two Legal Worlds

Co-founder of Morrow Ni LLP, Angus Ni runs what is almost certainly a one-of-a-kind U.S. practice: a small team handling complex commercial litigation across the country and around the world for Chinese clients caught up in cross-border disputes inside English-speaking legal systems. The work spans federal courtrooms, international arbitration panels, and the quieter rooms where settlements are negotiated.

Before launching the firm, he prosecuted securities class actions at Bernstein Litowitz Berger & Grossmann — the top shareholder rights firm in the U.S. — and earlier handled ICC and ICSID arbitrations as a litigator at Debevoise & Plimpton. That arc, from elite firm associate to lead trial counsel, shapes how he approaches every matter today.

His pro bono trial work in federal criminal court and his record in transnational commercial litigation have made him a recognized voice for Chinese-speaking clients navigating U.S. courts.
